Privacy Policy for Shadow Weaver

Last updated: 09 July, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PsychoReaper ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with the mobile game Shadow Weaver (the "App"). We built the App to be as privacy-respecting as possible: it does not require an account, does not connect to our own servers, and does not knowingly collect personal information from you.

Information We Collect

Shadow Weaver stores your game progress (levels completed, in-game currency, upgrade choices, and settings such as colour-blind mode) locally on your device only, using standard device storage. This data is never transmitted to us, and we do not have access to it. Uninstalling the App removes this data.

The App does not require you to create an account, and does not ask for your name, email address, or any other personally identifying information in order to play.

Third-Party Services

The App is distributed through the Google Play Store. Google may collect certain information (such as device identifiers and install data) as part of operating the Play Store and related services, independently of the App itself. This collection is governed by Google's own Privacy Policy, not by us.

As of the date above, the App does not integrate third-party advertising SDKs or analytics services. If this changes in a future update, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly before that update is released, and the Data Safety section of our Play Store listing will be updated to match.

Children's Privacy

Shadow Weaver is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. As noted above, the App does not collect personal information from any user in the first place. If we ever become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information (for example, through a support email), we will take steps to delete it.

Your Rights (California — CCPA/CPRA, and CalOPPA)

Because the App does not collect, sell, or share personal information, there is generally nothing to request access to, correct, or delete under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA). If you have questions or a specific request relating to any information you believe we may hold, contact us using the details below and we will respond.

Your Rights (EEA/UK — GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area and United Kingdom: because the App does not process personal data on our servers, most GDPR data subject rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability) do not apply to information generated by simply playing the game, since that information never leaves your device. If you believe we hold any personal data about you (for example, from a support email you sent us), you may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion.

Data Retention

Locally stored game data persists only on your device until you uninstall the App or clear its data through your device settings. We do not retain a copy of this data ourselves.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the App's data practices change (for example, if we add analytics, ads, or online features in a future update). The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision, and material changes will be reflected here before the corresponding App update is released.
