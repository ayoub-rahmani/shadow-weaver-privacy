Privacy Policy for Shadow Weaver

Last updated: 11 July, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PsychoReaper ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with the mobile game Shadow Weaver (the "App"). Shadow Weaver is designed to be playable entirely offline and does not require an account. The App includes one optional, opt-in online feature — a leaderboard — described below. Everything else about the App (progress, currency, upgrades, settings) stays on your device only.

Information We Collect

Local, on-device data (always)

Shadow Weaver stores your game progress (levels completed, in-game currency, upgrade choices, and settings such as colour-blind mode) locally on your device, using standard device storage. This data is never transmitted to us, and we do not have access to it. Uninstalling the App removes this data.

Leaderboard data (optional, only if you choose to join)

The App includes an optional online leaderboard, ranked by an in-game currency ("Glimmer Dust"). It is entirely opt-in: the App is fully playable offline, and you can always choose "Skip" instead of joining.

If you choose to join the leaderboard, the App will ask you to enter a display name. That name, together with your Glimmer Dust score, is sent to and stored on our backend (Google Firebase / Cloud Firestore) so it can be shown on the public leaderboard alongside other players' entries. We do not ask for your real name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifying information — only the display name you choose to type in, which can be anything you like.

The leaderboard is public: any player using the leaderboard feature can see the display names and scores of other participating players. Please don't use your real name or any identifying information as your display name if you'd prefer it not to be publicly visible.

We do not currently offer an in-app way to delete a leaderboard entry once created. If you'd like a leaderboard entry removed, contact us using the details at the bottom of this page and we will remove it.

Third-Party Services

The App is distributed through the Google Play Store. Google may collect certain information (such as device identifiers and install data) as part of operating the Play Store and related services, independently of the App itself. This collection is governed by Google's own Privacy Policy, not by us.

The optional leaderboard feature is powered by Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore), which stores the display name and score data described above on our behalf. Firebase's handling of this data is governed by Google's Firebase privacy and security terms in addition to this policy. Data sent to Firebase is encrypted in transit.

The App does not integrate third-party advertising SDKs or analytics services. If this changes in a future update, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly before that update is released, and the Data Safety section of our Play Store listing will be updated to match.

Children's Privacy

Shadow Weaver is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The optional leaderboard only collects a self-chosen display name and score — not a real name or other identifying information — but it is still not intended for use by children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has joined the leaderboard or otherwise provided us with personal information (for example, through a support email), we will take steps to delete it.

Your Rights (California — CCPA/CPRA, and CalOPPA)

Most of the App's data never leaves your device, so there is generally nothing to request access to, correct, or delete under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA). If you have joined the optional leaderboard, you may contact us using the details below to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your leaderboard display name and score.

Your Rights (EEA/UK — GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area and United Kingdom: game progress and settings generated by simply playing never leave your device, so most GDPR data subject rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability) do not apply to that information. If you have joined the optional leaderboard, the display name and score associated with your entry is personal data processed by us via Firebase, and you may contact us to request access, correction, erasure, or portability of that data.

Data Retention

Locally stored game data persists only on your device until you uninstall the App or clear its data through your device settings. We do not retain a copy of this data ourselves. If you join the optional leaderboard, your display name and score are retained on our Firebase backend until you request deletion (see above) or until the leaderboard feature is discontinued.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the App's data practices change (for example, if we add analytics, ads, or further online features in a future update). The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision, and material changes will be reflected here before the corresponding App update is released.
